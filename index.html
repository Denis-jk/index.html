Gender-Based Violence

Gender-based violence is a serious issue affecting millions of women and girls around the world. It includes physical, emotional, and psychological harm caused due to gender inequality.

Most cases happen at home, often out of fear, pressure, or control from a partner or family member. Every girl and woman has the right to live in safety, make decisions about her life, and speak out against abuse.

As a woman, you must know your rights and responsibilities in society. Most gender-based violence (GBV) happens in homes where the woman is not educated.
